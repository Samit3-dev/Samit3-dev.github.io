About Me

I am a Pakistani-American student with a strong interest in pursuing a career in the dental field. Growing up in a community where access to quality dental care is not always equitable has shaped my passion for improving oral health education and accessibility. I am currently a student at DeWitt Clinton High School and will graduate in 2026. Through my academic experiences and extracurricular involvement, I have developed a strong work ethic, leadership skills, and a commitment to serving others. My long-term goal is to become a dentist and work toward improving dental health systems in underserved communities.

Internship Overview
